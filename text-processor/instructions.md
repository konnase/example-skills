# Text Processor Instructions

## Sentiment Analysis
To perform sentiment analysis, look for emotional keywords:
- Positive: happy, great, excellent, love, improve, success.
- Negative: sad, bad, poor, hate, decline, failure.

## Summarization
To summarize, identify the first two sentences and the last sentence of the provided text.

## Keyword Extraction
Extract words that are capitalized and not at the beginning of a sentence.
